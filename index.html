Introduction

The Quantum Information Science practice website offers a brief quiz on prerequisite material, along with a collection of practice problems. The website covers a wide range of topics, which are listed below.

Quiz: Do I have the Prerequisites?

Topics: Eigenvalues and Eigen vectors, determinant, matrix inverses, Taylor series, and matrix exponents.

Classical Probability

Topics: Reverse processes, state vectors, stochastic matrix compilation, and code compilation/transpilation.

Quantum Probability Amplitude

Topics: Stochastic and unitary matrices, global phase, classical probabilities out of quantum amplitudes.

Measurements

Topics: In the ''Z basis'', in the ''X basis'', a comlete basis, consecutive measurements.
