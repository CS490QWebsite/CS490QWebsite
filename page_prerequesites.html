\[ \begin{aligned} A v=\lambda v & \Rightarrow(A-\lambda I) v=0 \\ & \Rightarrow \operatorname{Det}(A-\lambda I)=0 \end{aligned} \] 1. \[(1-\lambda)^2=0 \Rightarrow \lambda=1\] 2. \[\left|\begin{array}{cc}-\lambda & 1 \\ 1 & -\lambda\end{array}\right|=0 \Rightarrow \lambda^2-1=0 \Rightarrow \lambda=-1,1\] 3. \[\left|\begin{array}{cc}-\lambda & -i \\ i & -\lambda\end{array}\right|=0 \Rightarrow \lambda^2+i^2=0 \Rightarrow \lambda=-1,1\]
